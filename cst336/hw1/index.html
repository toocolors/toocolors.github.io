Introduction to Programming Languages

Computers have taken over the modern world. They are used in every field imaginable, for every task imaginable.
They are able to do this, in part, because of the programming languages that are used to give them instructions.

Countless languages exist. Some aim to provide speed, while others provide robust features.
Some are designed for small or focused programs, while others are designed to tackle large-scale projects.

This website aims to give an overview of the different types of languages, some examples of specific languages, and some examples "Hello World" programs, a simple program used to show some of a language's syntax.

You can click on the links at the top of the page or at the bottom to navigate to each page.
